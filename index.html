Tom Yum Goong: A Spicy Thai Delight

Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and aromatic spices. This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves, combined with succulent shrimp.

"The key to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors sweet, sour, salty, and spicy."
-Chef Somtai

For a Tom Yum Goong to be made here are the ingredients


Ingredients

1 cup of Thai basil

3 leaves of Kaffir lime leaves

2 stalks of Lemongrass

500g of shirmp


Preparation

1. Simmer the broth for 20 minutes

2. Add lemongrass, afir and Kaffir lime leaves

3. Stir in the shrimp and cook until pink

4. Season with the fish sauce, lime juice, and chili paste

5. Garnish with Thail basil and serve hot

Cooking Tips

  • 1/4 cup of fish sauce to add intensity
  • 1 tablespoon of chili paste gives the red soup its signature heat
